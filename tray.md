## Role: VP of Engineering
## Dates: 7/2022 - 3/2024

## Company Overview

Tray.io (now tray.com) is an integration and automation platform that enables businesses to connect their cloud applications and automate complex workflows. The platform provides a visual workflow builder and API integration layer used by enterprise customers.

---

## Key Accomplishments

- Joined and helped transition the platform from **monolith to microservices**

- Wrote the **first 3 microservices** and created the microservice platform that others built on

### Hiring Process Overhaul
- Made major changes to the hiring process
- Increased the rejection rate from **16% to significantly higher**, raising the engineering quality bar
- Improved the caliber of engineering hires across the organization

### Engineering Quality & TDD
- Moved the organization to having **70% TDD coverage** on all new microservices
- Established testing culture and practices across engineering teams
